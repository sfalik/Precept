# Brand Research

This folder is for brand research owned by the brand domain.

Use it for evidence and synthesis that inform brand decisions, such as:

- positioning studies
- README and hero research
- visual identity precedent analysis
- voice and tone studies
- structure proposals for brand artifacts

Do not use this folder for raw source captures or one-off artifact critique.

- Source material belongs in `../references/`.
- Critiques of specific artifacts belong in `../reviews/`.
- Approved rules belong in the brand spec or other brand-owned source-of-truth files.
